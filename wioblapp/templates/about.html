Our Goals

#1

To promote, organize, supervise, and stimulate interest and participation of summer basketball for boys and girls, men and women in an outdoor environment.

#2

To encourage good sportmanship and enjoyment of the basketball game.

#3

To encourage everyone to play basketball. All skill levels from beginner to intercity are welcome!

#4

To play an active role in our community and promote a healthy lifestyle through sports.
